Book Reviews

Ecclesiastes

I recommend Ecclesiastes because it gives a lot of insight about the natural workings of the world. King Solomon, the writer of the book, wanted to express how the pursuit of even the greatest worldly treasures is vanity. The solution is instead to look at things on this world less as goals, but more as gifts from God.

Romans

Romans is ultimately the book you want to read if you want a good overview of what Christianity is. It teaches us the Christian progression of justification, sanctification, and then glorification. It is one of the most exciting books of the Bible and personally has been life-changing for me!
